Vulture

A vulture is a bird of prey that scavenges on carrion. A particular characteristic of many vultures is a bald, unfeathered head. This bare skin is thought to keep the head clean when feeding, and also plays an important role in thermoregulation.

Akita

The Akita is a large breed of dog originating from the mountainous regions of Japan. There are two separate varieties of Akita: a Japanese strain, commonly called Akita Inu or Japanese Akita, and an American strain, known as the Akita or American Akita.

Panther

The Panther is a large member of the Big Cat family, native to Asia, Africa and the Americas. A black panther is the melanistic colour variant of the leopard and the jaguar. Black panthers of both species have excess black pigments, but their typical rosettes are also present.
